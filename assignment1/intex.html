Burning Love

Elvis Presley • 1972 • Rock / Soul

About the Song

“Burning Love” is a high-energy Elvis track from the early 1970s, known for its fast rhythm, bold vocals, and dramatic rock-and-soul feel.

The lyrics describe intense passion like a fire that keeps rising—powerful, uncontrollable, and impossible to ignore.
